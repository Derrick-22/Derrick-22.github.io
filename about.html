About


Ouimet Country Catering is a local business operating in the
areas surrounding Nipigon, Ontario, Canada.




We have over 20 years of Catering experience and are dedicated to bringing you a
great home style meal. With nineteen dollars per person for the dinner menu, and fourteen
dollars per person for the lunch menu. We offer a good meal for a reasonable price.
