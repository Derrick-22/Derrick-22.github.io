Welcome to Ouimet Country Catering



We are here to meet your catering needs, whether it is a wedding, a graduation, a business lunch, or
parties. We serve our brand of homestyle cooking. From a roast beef with mashed potatoes and gravy, to
fried chicken with coleslaw and corn. We have a variety of options and combinations that will please your tastebuds.





We also have a variety of delicious deserts. With traditional holiday treats like our fantastic pumpkin pie,
to our rich chocolate swirl cheesecake. Our mouth-watering deserts are a perfect way to end a meal.
